A Smart Email Client to Help User
Identify Malicious URLs

Background

With the popularity of the Internet, email has become a popular way for people to communicate. Some criminals begin to use email to cheat and attack email users include individuals and companies, by inserting malicious URLs (Malicious Uniform Resource Locators). In this project, we aim to build a smart, web-based email client which not only includes the basic functionalities of an email client but also has the ability to read through the contents of the emails and perform URLs detection based on machine learning technologies.

Methodology

The final product of the email client in this project is a lightweight, web-based application. The system architecture for the email client is provided in the left figure. For more details, please refer to the product plan document provided in Documentation.
